About Us

Flowers by Amel is dedicated to providing high-quality floral design for clients who seek a simple & elegant design. We intend to make enough profit to generate a fair return for our company and to support farmers to finance continued growth and development in quality products. We also maintain a friendly, fair, and creative work with reasonable price for all types of occasion, which respects diversity, new ideas and hard work.

At Flowers by Amel Flowers we can custom design floral arrangements to meet your needs and desires or you may simply put your trust in us to 'wow' you with an arrangement that will attract the attention of your friends and colleagues.

Our prices are quite reasonable and we hope you'll find them to be lower than other floral providers, prices do fluctuate depending on the time of year and the demand of upcoming holidays. Let us know what your floral needs are and we will give you the fairest estimate possible, and happily work around the scheduling of your event, delivery is free of charge.

Flowers by Amel is located in Bothell, Washington and caters to the greater Seattle area.
